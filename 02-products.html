Crownstone

DoBots offers an interesting component to a Smart Building, the crownstone, a Bluetooth Low-Energy switching block that can be embedded everywhere in a building. Not only can people use their smartphones to control every wire in the building, but also the building knows where the people are.

Read more at the page about the crownstone.



Guidestone

If you're only interested in the localization functionality of the Crownstone, we have a solution exactly for that, the Guidestone. The Guidestone is an iBeacon/Eddystone product that can be plugged into a power outlet. This means that it doesn't need a battery. Thanks to the fact that the power comes directly from the power outlet, it can send according to Apple specifications (every 100ms) and with considerable strength. Compared to battery-based iBeacon products this means that the localization can be done with faster response times and higher accuracy.

Read more at the page about the guidestone.
