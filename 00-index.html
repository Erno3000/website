Internet of Things

Nowadays, internet of things is a buzzword. At Crownstone we are actually making it a reality!

The Crownstone product line are physical products that bring the internet to everyday appliances. For example by integrating smartphone technology such as Bluetooth with power outlets it is possible to have common devices such as a television box turn off automatically when you're not home, or your mixer won't work when your kid is playing with it when you're not around.

  • Smart buildings

    The Crownstone is an essential building block in smart buildings. It can save energy by turning off office appliances when no one is around, but it can help as well with emergency response, flexible working, and many other office functions.
